And $\phi$ is called antecedent and $\psi$ is consequent. The truth table of $\phi\implies\psi$ as follows: $\phi$ $\psi$ $\phi\implies\psi$ T T T T F F F T T F F T The T denotes the truth and F denotes the false. So we cannot have $\phi$ is T and $\psi$ is F if $\phi\implies\psi$ is T. Hence $\phi\implies\psi$ must be F.
